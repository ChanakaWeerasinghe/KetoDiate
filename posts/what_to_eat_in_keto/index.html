What to eat

August 8, 2021

Foods to be discarded:

  1. Sugar and all kinds of sweets (marmalade, marshmallows, sweets, cake, ice cream).
  2. Cereals (wheat, rice, barley).
  3. Vegetable oil.
  4. (berries are allowed, in a minimum amount);
  5. Legumes (green bean pods are allowed);
  6. Root vegetables (beets, carrots, potatoes);
  7. Dietary options, for example, low-fat cottage cheese;
  8. Sauces, gravy containing starch, flour;
  9. Alcoholic drinks.

Ideal foods for diet:

  1. Meat and all its types;
  2. Seafood, including fish;
  3. Eggs
  4. Vegetables containing a minimum of carbohydrates (bell pepper, eggplant, tomatoes);
  5. Dairy products (cheese, butter, fatty cottage cheese);
  6. Nuts
  7. Mushrooms;
  8. Oils (coconut, olive);
  9. Spices, spices.
  10. Healthy ketogenic snacks
  11. Given the fact that the keto diet does not provide for calorie counting, snacks are absolutely acceptable. But it is important to remember that they must also be correctly selected. Permitted:
  12. Boiled eggs;
  13. Hard cheese;
  14. Seeds, nuts;
  15. Berries, for example, strawberries with cream;
  16. Yogurts;
  17. Tasty, healthy salads from permitted vegetables.
